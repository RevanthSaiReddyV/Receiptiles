How It Works

Setup in minutes.
Works forever.

No app downloads. No cashier training. No customer sign-ups. Just plug in and go.

No app download required
Customers use their existing Apple Wallet or Google Wallet — nothing new to install.
Step 1

Plug In

Swap your printer cable for the Receiptiles adapter. Takes 2 minutes — no software, no IT department.

Step 2

Instant Digitization

The adapter automatically converts every transaction into a clean, organized digital receipt in real time.

Step 3

Customer Taps

The customer holds their phone near the terminal. Their wallet opens automatically — just one tap to confirm.

Step 4

Saved Forever

The receipt lives in their Apple Wallet or Google Wallet — organized, searchable, and always available.
