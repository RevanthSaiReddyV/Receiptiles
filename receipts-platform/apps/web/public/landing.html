The Closed Loop

One tap. Done.

No cashier changes. No complicated accounts. A simple, organic ecosystem loop in three natural steps.

1. Connect Hardware

The Receiptiles dongle plugs directly into your existing register printer slot. It looks and acts like an ordinary printer.

2. Local Translation

Our onboard microchip extracts raw transaction streams locally, sorting them immediately into digital receipts.

3. Immediate Wallet Delivery

The customer simply taps their phone near the checkout screen. A beautiful system-native wallet receipt appears immediately.
